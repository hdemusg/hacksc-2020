Purpose

This website is dedicated to helping you find support by matching you with others who have gone through, or are currently going through, similar struggles. This should not be treated as a substitute for getting proffesional help, but rather a suplemental offering additional support. We believe that one of the best ways to improve your mental wellbeing is to hear the stories of others and know that you're not alone.
